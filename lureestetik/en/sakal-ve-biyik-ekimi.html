    • Beard and Mustache Transplantation

    One of the symbols of strong and aesthetic appearance in men is a bushy mustache and beard. Genetic factors determine the density of hair and beard in men. 3-4 years after entering puberty, the hairs in this area reach their ideal form.

    Beard transplantation and mustache transplantation can be done from the age of 20-22 when the hormones settle at the basal level.

    he follicles to be transplanted can be taken from the area just above the neck between the two ears or from above the ears. Within a week after transplantation, the skin is restored. Between 8 and 10 weeks after transplantation, the beard or mustache in the planted area becomes indistinguishable from the existing beard or mustache.
