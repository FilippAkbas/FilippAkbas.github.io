    • FUE Method

    FUE Method is the abbreviation of the term “Folicular Unit Extraction”.

    It is the most advanced hair transplant technique still used today.

    FUE Hair Transplant; It is the transfer of follicles containing healthy hair follicles taken from the hairy area between the two ears and above the ears with the help of a micro-motor to the balding area.

    It is a preferred method for the comfort of patients. The FUE Method does not leave a permanent scar in the area where the hair follicle is taken. You will not feel pain during the hair transplant procedure as it is performed with local anesthesia. The total hair transplantation period takes approximately 6-8 hours.

    Advantages of FUE Method

  • It is the most modern method currently used in terms of medical technology,
  • It is the most comfortable method for both the patient and the doctor,
  • There is no trace after the procedure in the area where the hair follicles are removed,
  • Recovery of operation sites within 24 to 48 hours,
  • It is performed with local anesthesia and the patient has no or very low postoperative
  • Possibility to use even a very short haircut after hair transplantation since it does not leave any traces,
  • Providing the opportunity to take 4500-5000 and more hair follicles in a single session depending on the condition of the area where hair follicles will be taken, (10000-15000 hair strands)
  • Providing the opportunity to get results in one session depending on the condition of the area where the hair follicle will be taken and the area to be transplanted,
  • It offers you the opportunity to return to your social life from the first day following the operation.
