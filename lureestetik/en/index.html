    Hair Transplant Process

    How Does Hair Transplantation Process Take Place?

    DAY 1
    Our guests arriving at the airport in Istanbul are welcomed with our VIP vehicle and our company hostess to accompany them.
    Our guests are transferred to our 4 or 5 star luxury hotel.
    DAY 2
    Blood tests and hair analysis are done in the morning.
    While the hair structure is getting analyzied, the most suitable hair transplant technique and design suitable for the face line is planned for our guest.
    By applying local anesthesia, the hair transplantation process is started, which will last between 6 and 8 hours.
    After the hair transplant is completed, special pillows and medicines are provided for the comfort of our guest's sleep and our guest is sent off to his/her hotel.
    DAY 3-4
    Our guests taken from the hotel by VIP vehicles are transferred to the hospital.
    A detailed examination of the hair transplantation process is made.
    The first hair washing process is carried out in the presence of our experts.
    Our guests are informed about how to care for their hair in the next process.
    Our guests are picked up from the hotel by VIP vehicle in accordance with the flight time, and dropped off at the airport.
